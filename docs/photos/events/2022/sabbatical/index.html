31 Things I Did with 31 Days Off of Work

Adobe grants all employees with a paid sabbatical every five years. I scheduled mine so that I would be away from work for the entire month of October. My plan included a lot of chores but also a lot of small activities that I enjoy. The goal was to focus on my mental and physical health by finishing some burdensome projects while making time for exercise and fun activities that I struggle to fit in to my normal schedule. So here are 31 of the highlights from my 31 days off.

1 - Started this online photo journal​

27 - Took a scenic drive​

28 - Played in the dirt​

31 - Completed a whole30 to learn about the impacts of different foods on my body.​
