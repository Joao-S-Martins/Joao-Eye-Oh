Adobe grants all employees with a paid sabbatical every five years. I scheduled mine so that I would be away from work for the entire month of October.
My plan included a lot of chores but also a lot of small activities that I enjoy. The goal was to focus on my mental and physical health by finishing some burdonsome projects
while making time for excercise and fun activities that I struggle to fit in to my normal schedule. So here are 31 of the highlights from my 31 days off.
